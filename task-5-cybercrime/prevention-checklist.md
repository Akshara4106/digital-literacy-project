Stay Safe Online – Prevention Tips

- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
- Always verify the sender before responding
- Use strong and unique passwords
- Enable two-factor authentication
- Avoid sharing personal or financial information online
- Check website URLs before entering details
- Use secure networks and avoid public Wi-Fi for transactions
- Keep your software and antivirus updated

Reporting:
- National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
